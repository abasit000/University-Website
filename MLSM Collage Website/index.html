About MLSM Collage

Maharaja Lakshman Sen Memorial (MLSM) College in Sundernagar, Himachal Pradesh, was established in 1976 by Raja Lalit Sen of Suket. The college was founded to honor his father, Maharaja Lakshman Sen, and the Raja donated the palace building along with 100 bighas of land to promote higher education in the state.
