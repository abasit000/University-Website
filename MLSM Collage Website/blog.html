Certificate & Online Programs

MLSM College offers various certificate and online programs. They have a range of courses in fields such as business, technology, and health, which can be completed online. If you're interested in a specific program or need more details, it's best to check their official website or contact their admissions office for the most current information.


MLSM College provides a variety of online and certificate programs in areas like:
Business and Management: Courses in business administration, marketing, and management.
Technology and IT: Programs in computer science, information technology, and cybersecurity.
Health and Wellness: Certifications related to healthcare management, medical billing, and health administration.
Education and Training: Programs focused on teaching strategies and educational leadership.

About MLSM Collage

Maharaja Lakshman Sen Memorial (MLSM) College in Sundernagar, Himachal Pradesh, was established in 1976 by Raja Lalit Sen of Suket. The college was founded to honor his father, Maharaja Lakshman Sen, and the Raja donated the palace building along with 100 bighas of land to promote higher education in the state.
