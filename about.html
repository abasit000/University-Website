About Us

Maharaja Lakshman Sen Memorial Collage

Maharaja Lakshman Sen Memorial (MLSM) College in Sundernagar, Himachal Pradesh, was established in 1976 by Raja Lalit Sen of Suket. The college was founded to honor his father, Maharaja Lakshman Sen, and the Raja donated the palace building along with 100 bighas of land to promote higher education in the state. The college, affiliated with Himachal Pradesh University (HPU), offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, including degrees in Science, Arts, Commerce, Business Administration, Computer Applications, and various Master's programs such as Physics, Chemistry, and Zoology. MLSM College is well-regarded for its emphasis on combining tradition and modernity, and its mission focuses on nurturing social consciousness and adapting to changing times.
